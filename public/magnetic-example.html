Magnetic Layer

Premium animations with magnetic cursors, antigravity transitions, and ambient effects. No dependencies, pure vanilla JavaScript.

Features

Magnetic Cursor

Premium dual-ring cursor that responds to your movement with spring physics. Expands on hover and creates ripple effects on click.

Antigravity Transitions

Smooth page transitions with staggered exit animations and wipe panel effects. Elements float upward with blur as you navigate.

Scroll Animations

Elements animate in as they enter the viewport. Supports staggered children, parallax scrolling, and custom timing.

Ambient Effects

Subtle background canvas with drifting gradient orbs. Responds to cursor position and creates depth without distraction.

Zero Dependencies

Pure vanilla JavaScript, no frameworks or libraries required. Lightweight (~8KB), fast, and works on all modern browsers.

Non-Invasive

Drop it into any project without modifying HTML or breaking existing functionality. Respects accessibility and mobile devices.
